ABOUT

The next January 21, 22 and 23 we gather the hackers that develop sustainable solutions to challenges launched

What's PoliHacks?

PoliHacks in this 2022 edition will focus on searching solutions through code to try to solve our increasing climate change problem, in matters of sustainability areas like social, environmental and economic.

Why was that theme chosen?

According to the global risk report 2020 published by the World Economic Forum, the catastrophic event with more impact and more likelihood to happen is the climate action failure, followed by extreme weather, biodiversity loss, natural disasters and human-made environmental disasters. In PoliHacks we're convinced that with creativity, inventiveness and technology we can face and win against this threatens, that's why we’re putting all our efforts together in organizing this hackathon in which besides having fun and build ourselves as professionals the young generations will have the opportunity of propose and implement solutions to this topic who will decide the course of our future.

GET IN

Why participate?

Be part of the movement that seeks to change the pessimistic picture of our future, help us think up impact solutions, and grow your professional career by adding this to your work portfolio. In addition, you’ll have the chance to win tremendous prizes and the visibility of our sponsors.

Who can participate?

Everybody is welcome, but this hackathon experience is designed mainly for university students worldwide who possess technical abilities and are close to finishing their studies or have just finished in the last year.

How will it be done?

Over three days the hackers will be immersed in a propitious atmosphere to stimulate ingenuity and creativity. We’ll have workshops before and during the event, and also integration activities so the participants can feel part of the community, furthermore recreational activities so attendees can rest their minds. Also, they'll be 24/7 spaces designated to networking and staff support for any issue that can appear.
